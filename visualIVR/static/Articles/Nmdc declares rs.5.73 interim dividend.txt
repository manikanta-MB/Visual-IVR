NMDC declares Rs. 5.73 interim dividend

Public sector mining major NMDC has posted a consolidated net profit of Rs. 2,048.4 crore for the quarter ended December from Rs. 2,108.05 crore in the year earlier. Revenue from operations increased almost 35% to Rs. 5,873.77 crore (Rs. 4,355.10 crore). NMDC has declared an interim dividend of Rs. 5.73 per equity share of face value of Rs. 1 each.
