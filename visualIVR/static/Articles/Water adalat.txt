BWSSB will hold water adalat on Thursday from 9.30 a.m. to 11 a.m.

It will be held in the following sub-divisions: East-2-3, East-1-2, South-2, South East 4, West 2, South West 2, South East 2, North West-3, Central 2, North 2, North East 1 and North Village 1. For issues regarding water supply, consumers can contact helpline number 1916 or WhatsApp number 8762228888, a release said.
