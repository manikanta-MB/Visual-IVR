The Narcotics Squad of the Central Crime Branch (CCB) on Tuesday busted a drug racket and arrested eight peddlers who were sourcing drugs from the dark net and paying through bitcoins.

The police seized MDMA and LSD blot papers worth Rs. 75 lakh from them.

The police raided two places in RMC Yard and Bandepalya and arrested the peddlers. The accused would sell the drugs to college students and employees of IT-BT companies, the police said.
