The Sadashivnagar police are on the lookout for two bike-borne men who stole a mobile phone from the wife of Congress MLA Priyank Kharge on Sunday.

Shruthi P Kharge was walking on the footpath of 8th main road around 7.15 am when the accused confronted her and before she could realise, the pillion rider snatched the mobile she was holding and the duo sped away.
