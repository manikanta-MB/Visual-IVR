India’s Smriti Mandhana gained two places to rise to fifth while Mithali Raj maintained the second spot among batters in the latest ICC Women’s ODI rankings on Tuesday.

Smriti has 710 rating points while Mithali has 738. Australia’s Alyssa Healy remained at the top with 742 points. Other Australians, Beth Mooney (719) and Amy Satterthwaite (717) are third and fourth respectively.

Among the bowlers, veteran Jhulan Goswami retained the second spot with 727 rating points, behind Australia’s Jess Jonassen (773).
