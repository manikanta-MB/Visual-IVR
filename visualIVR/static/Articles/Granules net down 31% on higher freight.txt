Granules net falls 31% on higher freight cost

Drugmaker Granules India reported consolidated net profit declined 31% to Rs. 101 crore for the December quarter on the back of increase in freight cost and lower gross margins. The lower net profit, compared with Rs. 147 crore in the year earlier, came on an 18% increase in income from operations to Rs. 997 crore (Rs. 845 crore). The company has declared an interim dividend of 25 paise per equity share of Rs. 1 each.
