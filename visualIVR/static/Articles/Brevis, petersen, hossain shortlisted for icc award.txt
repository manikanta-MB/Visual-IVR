Two South Africans — batter Keegan Petersen and record-breaking U-19 World Cup star Dewald Brevis — along with Ebadot Hossain of Bangladesh were on Tuesday nominated for the ICC Player of the Month Award following their stellar performances in January.

Among women candidates, Sri Lanka’s Chamari Athapaththu, West Indies’ Deandra Dottin and England’s Heather Knight were named as nominees for the ICC Women’s Player of the Month.
